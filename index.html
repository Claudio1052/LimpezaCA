Post-Construction Cleaning

After the builders leave, we come in. We remove dust, debris, and residues so your new or renovated space is truly ready to enjoy.

From Construction Site to Clean Space

Post-construction cleaning is a specialized service that requires the right techniques and products. Drywall dust, adhesive residue, paint splatter, and construction debris require more than a regular clean.

Our team is trained to safely remove all construction byproducts from every surface without damaging new finishes, floors, or fixtures.

We serve both residential renovations and commercial construction projects across Calgary and the surrounding areas.

What's Included

  • Removal of drywall dust and debris
  • Paint splatter cleaned from surfaces
  • All windows cleaned inside and out
  • New fixtures and appliances wiped and polished
  • Floors swept, vacuumed, and mopped
  • Cabinetry and countertops cleaned
  • Vents and ducts wiped of construction dust
  • Final touch-up and walkthrough
